my banga soup

    INGREDIENTS

  • 1500grams Palm fruit
  • 1 medium size Cat fish or any preferred fresh fish
  • Assorted meats of your choice
  • 2-3 medium pieces stockfish
  • 1-2 medium dried fish
  • 1/2 cup fresh or smoked shrimps(optional)
  • 1 tablespoon ground pepper
  • 1-2 tablespoon banga spice
  • 1-2 tablespoon crushed obeltientien or dried bitter leave
  • 1 oburunbebe strick
  • Bouillon cubes or knorr
  • salt to taste
  • crayfish

    cooking instructions

  • step 1

  • extract the palm fruit concentrate from the palm fruits.cut,clean and wash your fish thoroughly,especially if you are using catfish and wash you meat stock,cook the banga fruit and pound,then wash

  • step 2

  • cook the meat stock and fish with spice,cook till it's done.

  • step 3

  • set the pot of palm fruit extract on the stove and start cooking at high heat.leave to boil till you notice some red oil at the surface of the banga.if you think that the banga soup is watery.cook till the soup has thickened to the consistency you like for your soup.

    step 4

  • Now, add the already cooked fish and meat also add the spice,leave to boil well.

  • step 5

  • Add the dry bitter leaf ,leave to simmer for 2mins.the banga soup is done. serve with white rice or use the Delta style banga soup to eat with starch,garri,semolina,amala or pounded yam

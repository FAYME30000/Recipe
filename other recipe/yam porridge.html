INGREDIENTS

  • White yam (1kg)
  • Habanero pepper
  • 2 tablespoonful grounded crayfish
  • Smoked fish
  • 1 medium size onion
  • Red oil (palm oil)
  • 2 stock cube seasoning
  • salt to taste
  • Vegetables ( pumpkin or spinach)

    • Direction

  • Peel and cut the yam into desired cube sizes, wash and place in a pot
  • Wash and cut the onion and vegetables into tiny pieces.
  • Grind the pepper and debone the smoked fish.
  • Add enough water to cover the yam cubes and cook at a medium heat.
  • When the yam starts boiling, add the grounded crayfish, habanero pepper, onion, stock cubes, and palm oil.
  • Add salt and continue cooking until the yam is soft as desired
  • When the yam is cooked, add the vegetable and stir very well with a wooden spoon until the liquid thickens. Then serve.
